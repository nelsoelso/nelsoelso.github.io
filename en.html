Features

One Platform.
Many Capabilities.

From appointment booking to automated teaching records – BEAT connects everyone involved in clinical education.

📋

Digital Logbook

Every clinical activity is linked to the appropriate EPA. A structure that automatically meets NKLM requirements.

📅

Digital Appointment Coordination

Teachers post appointments, students book in real time. No emails, no phone calls, no overhead.

✅

Check-in & Attendance

Teachers check in participants – only checked-in persons can be assessed. Clear accountability.

🔄

Bidirectional Feedback

Students rate teaching quality. Teachers receive constructive feedback. Both sides grow.

🎙️

AI Dictation

Capture meaningful pedagogical feedback quickly by voice – powered by Whisper AI. Precise and fast.

📊

Hospital-wide Statistics

Teaching coordinators see department utilization, EPA distribution and feedback scores at a glance – exportable at any time.

🧠

AI Progress Reports

Automatically generated analyses and recommendations based on each student's individual EPA progress.

📈

Competency Tracking

Students see at any time which EPAs have been completed and where development potential exists.

🎓

Case Studies & Quizzes

Create case studies, answer MCQs, actively reflect on knowledge – spaced repetition built into the platform.
